Description

  • We visualize all the planets discovered in selected year. We can play animation to visualize year wise for all the years.
  • The size of circle is directly proportional to the radius of planet.
  • The color of the circle represents planet type, earth is sky blue, jupiter is orange.
  • Concentric dotted circles represent the radial distance in light years (scaled different for each year) from the earth.
  • Hovering over planets display tooltip, blurrs everything but earth and planet, clicking on them give mass visualization.
  • Mass scale is a novel visualization where we put # of earths/jupiters on one end whose mass is equivalent to 1 planet.
